Storyline

Lisa Roberts, a popular paranormal enthusiast devises a plan to become world famous by filming the conjuring of a Demon and posting it on the YouTube, in the hopes it will turn viral. With no real understanding of magick, Lisa inadvertently opens a portal to the Abyss. Now, Amy Roberts, Lisa's pragmatic and skeptical sister must gamble with her very own soul in an effort to fight off the demon that has possessed Lisa. Only with the assistance of Zayden Zang, a ghost busting internet sensation, does Amy stand a chance of casting out the demon. Together with Zayden and Melvin, the gothic nerd from next door, Amy combats devilish spirits for the exorcism of her sister and the salvation of their very own souls. Written by Anonymous
